小作文

申请信

Dear Mr.A（后省略此处）

I am writing to apply for the position as the personal secretary to the general manager. (the opportunity for further study in your university).

I am fully confident that I am the right candidate. To begin with, I major in secretary and will graduate from Peking University. I have a good academic performance in school and have been the top five students for four years in my grade. In addition, I am an open-minded, cooperative and ready-to-help girl.

Enclosed is my resume to provide additional information. Thank you for your time and consideration. I am looking forward to your reply at your earliest convenience.

Sincerely, Li Ming（后省略此处）

投诉信

I am deeply dissatisfied with my recent shopping experience in one of your retail stores, and ask you to step in and resolve the matter.

On the afternoon of 5 December, 2022, I visited your retail store at IFS mall, looking for a ___. A staff member recommended your latest model and assured me that its battery capacity was much larger than that of most products on the market. I was very impressed by this feature and bought it without hesitation. But as soon as I tested it out at home, I found out that the battery life actually showed no significant improvement. The next day, I brought the product back to the store and asked for a full refund. To my shock, that same staff member flatly denied that he made the false claim. And the store manager sided with him and rejected my refund request.

I will highly appreciate if you can take care of this dispute and get back to me as soon as possible.

建议信

I am writing the letter in purpose of drawing your attention to the fact that some people stiluse plastic bags in spite of restrictions on the use of them. and I will try to make some conducive recommendations concerning the write pollution.

In my humble opinion, to eradicate the phenomenon of widespread write pollution, it would be wise to take the following actions. Above all, the public should keep in mind to foster consciousness of the negative influence of frequent use plastic bags. Besides, there is no doubt that the authorities should strengthen the enforcement of the laws to completely ban the improper use of plastic bags.

I hope you will find these proposals useful, and I would be ready to discuss this matter with you to further details. Your prompt attention to my recommendations will be highly appreciated.

邀请信

I am writing on behalf of the English Department to invite you to give a lecture in our college.

We know that you are an expert on American literature. As English majors, our students would like to know something about American literature. We would be very grateful if you could give a talk on “Contemporary American Literature” to students of the English Department on Saturday, June 4. If this subject does not suit you, any other similar topic would be welcomed as well.

We have already had several very interesting talks from some distinguished visitors from various countries and we look
forward early to the opportunity to benefit from your experience and wisdom.

通知信

The first year in Foreign Languages School is drawing to a close. So far, we have been focusing on learning the skills in English listening and speaking. In order to further test your competence in spoken English, we beg to notify you that our school decides to organize an English Speech Contest.

The English Speech Contest is scheduled to be held at 2 p.m. on May 25 in the Lecture Hall of our school. The speech will be centered on the theme of language learning and culture. All the students are welcome to participate in the contest. The two foreign teachers, Professor Smith and Professor Johnson will be invited as the judges and the winners will be awarded an Oxford English Dictionary.

If you are interested in this activity, please feel free to contact us via xxxxxxx@163.com. The deadline is ___. Come and join us now!

第一段

  1. 漫画

    The cartoon depicts … Although he…, he …

    The cartoon depicts a young athlete on a running track. Although he has just crossed the finishing line, he immediately starts his next round without stopping to catch his breath

  2. 对比图

    In the upper picture, we learn that …. On the contrary, in the lower picture, ….

    The first picture show us .On the contrary, according to the second picture, we learn that…

  3. 表格类：(表格数据多，点到为止，只点首尾，中间的非典型数据不用提及)

    The table clearly reflects / reveals …. According to the data given above, we can conclude that…. is highest… In contrast, …. is the lowest …

  4. 曲线图：(曲线图的数据及术语要根据情况表达到位)

    The line chart / line graph clearly illustrates that the number of … increased / jumped from 时间 to 时间…

  5. 柱状图：(纵坐标一般为数据，横坐标一般是年代及主体，注意抓住不同主体描述)

    According to the above bar chart / column chart, the number / percentage of … from 时间 to 时间…

  6. 饼状图：(饼状图是所有图表中相对最简单的一种，只需要掌握占比多少的表达，描述清楚即可)

    The pie chart above clearly reveals the…in a certain city / university /… Among all the categories, the percentage of … is/are the highest，accounting for… On the contrary, the statistics of … take up merely.., the lowest…
